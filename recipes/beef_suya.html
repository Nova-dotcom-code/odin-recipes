Beef Suya Skewers

A spicy, smoky street food favorite made with thinly sliced beef marinated in a peanut-based spice mix. It's grilled to perfection and served with sliced onions and tomatoes. Suya is bold, flavorful, and deeply rooted in Nigerian food culture

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef (sirloin or flank steak), thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons ground peanuts (or peanut butter)
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Wooden skewers (soaked in water for 30 minutes if grilling)
