Jollof Rice

A beloved West African classic! This one-pot rice dish is cooked in a rich tomato and pepper sauce, seasoned with spices, and often served with fried plantains or grilled chicken. Itâ€™s a staple at Nigerian parties and family gatherings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2-3 tomatoes, blended (or 1 can of crushed tomatoes)
  • 1 red bell pepper, blended
  • 1-2 Scotch bonnet peppers (adjust to taste)
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder (optional)
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: protein of choice (chicken, beef, or fish)
