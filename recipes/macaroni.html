Classic Macaroni Salad

This classic macaroni salad is a perfect side dish for picnics and barbecues. It's creamy, tangy, and loaded with veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni, cooked and cooled
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard (optional, for a tangy kick)
  • ½ cup chopped dill pickles (or sweet pickles, if preferred)
  • ¼ cup finely diced red onion
  • ½ cup diced celery (for crunch)
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped (optional)
  • A splash of pickle juice (optional, for extra zing)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps for preparing Macaroni Salad

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked macaroni, mayonnaise, and mustard (if using).
  2. Add the chopped pickles, red onion, celery, and hard-boiled eggs (if using).
  3. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If you like, add a splash of pickle juice for extra flavor.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Serve chilled and enjoy your classic macaroni salad!
