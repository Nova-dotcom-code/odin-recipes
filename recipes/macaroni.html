Classic Macaroni Salad

This classic macaroni salad is a perfect side dish for picnics and barbecues. It's creamy, tangy, and loaded with veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni, cooked and cooled
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard (optional, for a tangy kick)
  • ½ cup chopped dill pickles (or sweet pickles, if preferred)
  • ¼ cup finely diced red onion
  • ½ cup diced celery (for crunch)
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped (optional)
  • A splash of pickle juice (optional, for extra zing)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
